The information in your health record has huge potential to save lives.

We are putting you in control

Your medical data is of critical importance in advancing health research. We are building a system that empowers patients to gain full control over use of their data, allowing them to choose the projects they want to support. Let's make it clear: no insurance company benefiting from your data, no ways to identify you, no access unless explicit consent is given for the intended purpose. Just curing and treating diseases.
